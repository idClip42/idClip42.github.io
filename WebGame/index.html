Welcome to Pizza Quest!

How to Play:

This is a point and click adventure game, in the style of 90s LucasArts adventure games.
Move your character by clicking around the screen.
Hovering the mouse over objects and characters displays their name in the UI.
Click on an Action Button and then an object or character to perform the desired action.
You can use objects with other objects, and give objects to people.

Examine everything and talk to everyone.

Walkthrough:

Intro:

Your goal in this game is to get into the Pizza Place and get a pizza.

Blocking your way is Agnes, who wants to prevent you from getting pizza.
In order to get her to move, you'll have to call her from a pay phone (your cell is dead).
This task is split into two parts: Getting her phone number and finding a quarter for the payphone.

You start at a bus stop, with a theater to your right and a parking lot with the Pizza Place and a Fitness Place to your left.

Getting Agnes' Phone Number:

Go left, to the parking lot, and walk to the Pizza Place.
Talk to the woman and learn what you can from her.

Now that you've met her, walk right to the Fitness Place and talk to Josh, the guy out front, about the woman.
You can now ask for her phone number. Josh will ask you a series of questions about Agnes, most of which you can answer based on your conversation with her.
You do not, however, know what year she won the Regional Soccer Tournament. You'll have to find that information elsewhere.
Ask Josh if he has any "free samples" and he'll give you a toy robot claw for some reason.

Head back past the bus stop to the theater and look in the trash. There's something interesting in there but your character refuses to reach in and grab it.
Instead, use the claw on the trash to get the thing out. It's a trophy!
Unfortunately, the trophy is old and dirty and unreadable. It needs to be washed.
If you go back to the bus stop, you'll find a storm drain with water in it.

Using the trophy with the storm drain will clean it off, and if you read it you'll be able to find out what year Agnes won the Tournament.
Armed with this information, return to Josh and convince him to give you the phone number.

Finding a Quarter:

Inside the theater, you'll find a man conspicuously flipping a coin.
If you ask him about it, you'll learn it's his lucky quarter and that he'll never part with it.
Tough luck. Push him, causing him to drop his coin and panic.
Though you can see where his coin landed on the floor (it's small but twinkly), you can't pick it up without him seeing.
You'll have to give him something to make him stop looking for it.

Look at the vending machine. The player will notice that someone left a soda bottle in it.
Pick up the soda bottle and open it. You'll spill it, but you'll end up with a cap that looks vaguely like a quarter.
Give the cap to the man. He is satisfied.
Pick up the quarter and be on your merry way.

Ending the game:

Once you have the phone number and the quarter, use the quarter on the pay phone in the theater to make a call.
After the call, make your way back to the pizza place, where Agnes is nowhere to be found.
Enter the pizza place and talk to the girl at the counter to order your pizza.
Once you have the pizza, walk over to the tables and enjoy sweet, sweet victory.
